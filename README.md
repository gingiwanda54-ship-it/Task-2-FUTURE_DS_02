# Task-2-FUTURE_DS_02
# 🩺 Kidney Hub – Patient Churn & Retention Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes patient retention and churn in a healthcare environment, modeled as a SaaS-style subscription business.

The goal is to identify:
- Why patients stop treatment (churn)
- Which segments are most at risk
- What drives long-term retention

---

## 🧠 Business Problem
Healthcare organizations face challenges similar to SaaS companies:
- High churn reduces long-term outcomes
- Patient engagement impacts retention
- Cost and access influence behavior

---

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset includes 5 tables:

- **accounts** – patient demographics  
- **subscriptions** – treatment lifecycle & revenue  
- **feature_usage** – engagement data  
- **support_tickets** – support interactions  
- **churn_events** – churn reasons  

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel  
- Power BI  

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed

### 🔹 Data Preparation (Excel)
- Cleaned and structured multi-table dataset  
- Standardized date formats and categories  
- Organized relational data using account_id  

---

### 🔹 Data Modeling (Power BI)
- Built relationships across 5 tables  
- Created a star schema model  
- Developed calculated measures (DAX)  

---

### 🔹 Churn & Retention Analysis
- Calculated churn rate  
- Identified high-risk segments  
- Analyzed retention trends  

---

### 🔹 Customer Segmentation
- By province  
- By payment type (Medical Aid vs Cash)  
- By treatment plan  

---

### 🔹 Engagement & Support Insights
- Measured feature usage impact on retention  
- Analyzed support satisfaction vs churn  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Cash-paying patients have higher churn rates  
- Patients in rural areas show shorter retention  
- Higher engagement leads to better retention  
- Cost and travel distance are major churn drivers
